About Us

For more than 15 years, we provide professional support to families to take care of elderly and adults with functional disabilities, cognitive impairment, and/or medical frailty who may require continuous assistance and/or supervision. We offer an ideal alternative to caregivers by providing a daytime care environment outside the home, which prevents premature placement in a nursing home. We are committed to improve the quality of life with recreational activities, social services, high-quality nutritious meals and expert nursing care in a safe and loving environment. Our primary focus to ensure that our clients achieve an overall improvement in their quality of life through the effective management of chronic conditions, cognitive and functional impairments as well as various comorbidities.
